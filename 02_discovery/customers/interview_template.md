## Interview Script

### Warm-up (2-3 min)

- Thank them for their time
- Brief intro (who you are, why you're talking)
- "I'm trying to learn about [topic], not sell you anything"
- Ask permission to take notes

---

### Context Questions (5 min)

_Understand their world before diving into problems._

- "Tell me about your role. What does a typical day/week look like?"
- "What are you responsible for?"
- "What tools/processes do you use for [relevant area]?"

---

### Problem Exploration (10-15 min)

_Focus on past behavior, not hypotheticals._

**The Mom Test Questions:**

✅ **Good Questions (Past/Specific):**
- "Tell me about the last time you [experienced problem]..."
- "What happened? Walk me through it."
- "What did you do about it?"
- "What else have you tried?"
- "How much time/money did that cost you?"
- "What was the hardest part?"

❌ **Bad Questions (Future/Hypothetical):**
- ~~"Would you use a product that..."~~
- ~~"How much would you pay for..."~~
- ~~"Do you think this is a good idea?"~~

---

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- "Is there anything else I should know about [topic]?"
- "Who else should I talk to about this?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions?"
- "Would you be interested in seeing what we build?"

# Stage 3: Company Building

> "First-time founders focus on product. Second-time founders focus on distribution." — Justin Kan

## Purpose

**Scale** — Build the organization to:
1. Grow the customer base
2. Grow the team
3. Build sustainable operations

---

## Key Activities

### Scale Operations

- [ ] Hire key roles
- [ ] Build management team
- [ ] Create organizational structure
- [ ] Implement systems and processes

### Scale Growth

- [ ] Double down on working channels
- [ ] Expand to new segments/markets
- [ ] Build brand

### Scale Product

- [ ] Expand product offering
- [ ] Build platform/ecosystem
- [ ] Maintain product/market fit

---

## Organizational Milestones

| Stage | Team Size | Key Hires | Focus |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Early | 1-10 | Founding team | Product + initial customers |
| Growth | 10-50 | First managers | Process + culture |
| Scale | 50-200 | Executives | Delegation + systems |
| Mature | 200+ | Full org | Efficiency + innovation |

---

## Key Metrics at Scale

| Category | Metric | Current | Target |
|----------|--------|---------|--------|
| **Revenue** | ARR/MRR | $ | $ |
| **Growth** | MoM/YoY | % | % |
| **Efficiency** | Revenue per employee | $ | $ |
| **Retention** | Net Revenue Retention | % | >100% |

---

## Common Scaling Challenges

| Challenge | Symptom | Solution |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Culture dilution** | "It's not like the old days" | Document and reinforce values |
| **Communication breakdown** | Silos, misalignment | Regular all-hands, clear goals |
| **Process overhead** | Slow decision-making | Right-size process for stage |
| **Technical debt** | Slow releases | Dedicated time for infra |

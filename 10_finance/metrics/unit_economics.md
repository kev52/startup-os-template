# Unit Economics

> "If the unit economics don't work, growth just makes you run out of money faster." — Marc Andreessen

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| **CAC** | Total acquisition cost / New customers | $ | $ |
| **LTV** | ARPU × Gross Margin × Avg Lifetime | $ | $ |
| **LTV:CAC** | LTV / CAC | :1 | >3:1 |
| **Payback Period** | CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) | mo | <12 mo |
| **Gross Margin** | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | % | % |

## Ritual Formats

### Daily Standup

**Format:**
1. What did I do yesterday?
2. What am I doing today?
3. Any blockers?

**Rules:**
- Start on time
- Keep to 15 minutes
- Take deep dives offline

### Weekly Team Sync

**Agenda:**
1. Wins (5 min)
2. Metrics review (10 min)
3. Priorities for the week (15 min)
4. Blockers and discussions (20 min)
5. Action items (5 min)

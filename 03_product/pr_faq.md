# PR/FAQ (Press Release / Frequently Asked Questions)

> "Start by writing the press release. Work backwards from that." — Jeff Bezos

## What is PR/FAQ?

Before writing code, Amazon teams write the press release announcing the product launch — **as if it already exists**. This forces clarity on the customer benefit, not the technology.

---

# [Product Name]

## Internal Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**[City], [Date]** — [Company Name] today announced [Product Name], [one sentence that captures the key customer benefit].

### Headline

> **[One line that captures the customer benefit — what would make a customer stop and read?]**

### Subheadline

> [Who is this for] can now [what they can do] [key differentiator].

---

### Opening Paragraph (The Problem)

[Describe the customer and their problem. Make it vivid and specific. The reader should feel the pain.]

### The Solution

[Product Name] [solves the problem by doing X]. [Key capability 1], [key capability 2], and [key capability 3] — all in [key differentiator like "one click" or "real-time"].

### Customer Quote

> "[Quote from a fictional customer expressing their delight]"
> — [Name], [Role] at [Company Type]

### How It Works

1. **[Step 1]** — [Simple description]
2. **[Step 2]** — [Simple description]
3. **[Step 3]** — [Simple description]

### Availability

[Product Name] is available [when/where]. [Pricing model]. Get started at [website].

### Company Quote

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Customer FAQ

**Q: What is [Product Name]?**

**Q: Who is this for?**
A: [Target customer description]

A: [Key differentiators]

**Q: How much does it cost?**

**Q: What if it doesn't work for me?**

### Internal FAQ

**Q: Why now? Why didn't we build this before?**
A: [Market timing, technology enablers]

**Q: What's the biggest risk?**
A: [Honest assessment and mitigation]

**Q: What are we NOT building?**
A: [Explicit scope limitations]

**Q: How will we know if it's working?**
A: [Success metrics]

**Q: How long will this take to build?**
A: [Timeline estimate]

**Q: What's the business model?**
A: [Revenue strategy]

---

## PR/FAQ Review Checklist

- [ ] **Customer-centric:** Is it about what the customer can do, not what we built?
- [ ] **Clear value prop:** Can someone unfamiliar understand the benefit in 10 seconds?
- [ ] **Specific:** Are we using concrete examples, not vague descriptions?
- [ ] **Honest:** Have we addressed the hard questions in the Internal FAQ?
- [ ] **Exciting:** Would a customer/press actually care about this announcement?

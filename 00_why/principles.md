## Decision-Making Framework

When facing a tough decision, ask:

1. **Does it align with our WHY?** (See `why.md`)
2. **Does it move us toward our vision?** (See `vision.md`)
3. **Does it honor our principles?** (This document)
4. **What would we do if we weren't afraid?**
